About

FarragDesignHouse

is an Egyptian firm specialized in architecture &
interior design located in cairo.

Our philosophy

Innovative solutions with high quality standards mixed with rich textures always our way to turn your dream house into real spaces. Crossing the boundaries of time, functionality, and luxury.

Our approach

We always listen to the needs of our clients and how their normal day goes on until we reach to a design combined with functionality and luxurious look. As well as we are very lenient with changing the design multiple time until we reach to the perfect one that serves our customers needs on the highest level.
